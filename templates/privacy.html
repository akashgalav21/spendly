Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you use Spendly, we collect the following information:

  • Account information: your name and email address provided at registration.
  • Expense data: amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions you enter.
  • Usage data: basic logs of actions taken within the app (e.g., login timestamps).

2. How We Use Your Data

We use the data we collect solely to provide and improve the Spendly service:

  • To display your expenses and generate spending summaries.
  • To authenticate you and keep your account secure.
  • To diagnose technical issues and improve app performance.

We do not use your data for advertising and we do not sell it to third parties.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored on secured servers. We take reasonable technical measures to protect it from unauthorised access, loss, or disclosure. You may delete your account at any time, which will permanently remove all your data from our systems.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly uses a small number of third-party services to operate:

  • Hosting infrastructure to serve the application.
  • Google Fonts to load the typefaces used in the interface.

These services may receive limited technical data (such as your IP address) as a normal part of internet traffic. They are not given access to your expense data.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out via the contact information provided in the app. We will respond to all reasonable requests within a reasonable timeframe.
